Weaknesses and next steps:

  • A few immediate next steps can be identified. For starters, it would be interesting to see year-over-year(YOY) trends. This would be a good metric for TTC as they can identify whether they are reducing the number of delays and the severity of delays. Furthermore, by understanding the distribution of the cause of the delays, TTC will be able to assess weaknesses in their own system. It could have implications on upgrading their trains and buses, increasing the number of back-up trains.

  • Covid-19 has inexplicably affected every part of our lives, transportation is no different.It would be interesting to see how some of the policies that have come about as a result of COVID-19. For example, has the disinfection process reduced the number of buses that can service a route? The reduced number of patrons might call for an increase in the number of vehicles that are in use, does this increase in number of vehicles translate to an increase in delays?
